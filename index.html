About Me

I am an incoming PhD student in the Department of Computer Science at the University of Utah, starting Fall 2025. My research interests lie in operating systems and systems security. Prior to this, I was a master's student at the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign, where I worked with Prof. Tianyin Xu.

Research

I am currently exploring topics related to resource scheduling and fairness in Linux kernel schedulers. My long-term goal is to design operating systems that adapt better to modern workloads and hardware.

Previously, I worked on recommender systems and MLOps infrastructure at a major media tech company.
